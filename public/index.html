About

A community forged for the next era of cybersecurity.

Nex Aura began with a simple belief — that the world's most curious minds deserve a place where they can learn ruthlessly, compete fairly, and build fearlessly. Today we are a collective of hackers, researchers, students and engineers. Tomorrow, we are the platform where the world sharpens its edge.

Who we are

A distributed community of security researchers, CTF players, red-teamers and blue-teamers united by craft over credentials.

Why we started

Because talent is everywhere, but access isn't. We built Nex Aura to remove the gate between curiosity and world-class practice.

Our mission

Train the next generation of defenders and offensive specialists through relentless, hands-on competition.

Our vision

A single home for competitive cybersecurity — where every discipline, from binary exploitation to AI security, has a stage.

What makes us different

We are practitioners, not spectators. Everything we ship — writeups, labs, tournaments — is built by the community for the community.

Culture

Curiosity without ego. Depth without gatekeeping. Excellence measured by what you can teach, not what you can hoard.

FAQ

Frequently asked.

Is Nex Aura free to join?+

Yes. The community, CTFs, writeups and events are free. The upcoming platform will have a generous free tier alongside premium seasonal passes.

Do I need experience to participate?+

No. We host beginner-friendly tracks, mentorship programs and guided learning paths. If you're curious, you belong here.

When is the platform launching?+

The Nex Aura Platform enters invite-only alpha this year, with a public beta and the first global championship to follow.

How is Nex Aura different from HTB, THM or CTFtime?+

We combine competitive infrastructure, community-authored content, AI security, and cross-discipline tournaments in one place — with a real ranked ladder and seasonal championships.

Can I contribute?+

Absolutely. Author challenges, publish writeups, mentor newcomers, build tools, or help run events. There's a place for every skill level.

Is Nex Aura a company?+

Nex Aura is a community first. A platform team supports the infrastructure that makes the community possible.
